About the Journal

Current Trends in Pharmaceutical & Clinical Research (CTPH) is an open access, peer-reviewed journal publishing research across clinical pharmacy, pharmaceutics, pharmacoeconomics, pharmacoepidemiology, and health policy. We aim to accelerate evidence translation to improve patient care worldwide.

Indexing & Policies

We follow COPE guidelines and use double-blind peer review. ISSN and DOIs are issued for published articles. We target DOAJ and Scopus indexing in future.

Open Access Peer Reviewed DOI (Zenodo)

Author Guidelines (short)

  • Article types: Original research, Review, Case report, Short communication
  • Structured abstract (≤300 words), max 6 keywords
  • References: Vancouver style preferred
  • All authors must provide ORCID and conflict of interest statement
